About Me

Hello! My name is Olinda Cadena-Thrower and I currently work in IT Operations. I spend most of my day fixing medical software and flight software for helicopters.

When I am not in class, or fixing helicopters, I am at the gym, patrolling as part of my local police department, or reading.

Previously I have worked at Apple HQ, and Cinemark HQ doing various technical jobs with minimal coding knowledge required. I worked primarily with servers, maintaining and troubleshooting them, and completed a variety of package building for machine reimaging and mass updates.

I am learning coding so that I can grow my skills and start on a new career path.
